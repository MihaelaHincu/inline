My education is..a long story

I could say that I've chosen the much harder route of self-education, as it has always fit me better anyway. I don't think I would do well in the college format of learning. I prefer to dive deep into one thing, rather than spread my learning out across multiple subjects at once. I'm the type that circles back and fills in the blanks when they become relevant to what I'm doing. I like to enroll in informal courses. For me, the certifications I achieve out of the courses represent: dedication, hard work, and perseverance, because to me they weren't easy. They required me to plan and see the big picture.
     I don't know why it makes me so happy to create something out of thin air keys, but it does, and I love it.

The final exam consisted of a presentation of a self-made one-page website. It concluded with an ANC accreditation for "webpages designer".
